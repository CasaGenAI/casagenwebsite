Website Documentation: CasaGen Public-Facing Site
1. Primary Goal:
To convert visitors into registered users by clearly communicating the platform's unique value: combining AI-powered lead generation, investment analysis, and transaction management into one simple platform.

2. Target Audience:

Individual Real Estate Agents (tech-savvy, looking for an edge)

Real Estate Investors (data-driven, focused on ROI)

Brokerage Teams (focused on productivity and team management)

3. Key Conversion Points (CTAs):

Primary: "Start Free Trial" or "Sign Up"

Secondary: "Request a Demo" or "Watch Video"

4. Sitemap (Website Structure):

/ (Homepage)

/features (A detailed page with sub-sections)

/pricing (Tiered pricing plans)

/blog (For content marketing, SEO, and news)

/contact (A simple contact/support form)

/login (This links to the portal, not part of the marketing site)

/signup (This is the registration page)

Homepage Wireframe (A Modern, Narrative Structure)
This is a top-to-bottom blueprint for your homepage. A modern website tells a story as the user scrolls.

[Section 1: The Hero]
Goal: Explain what your product is and why it matters in 5 seconds. This is the most important section.

Key Components:

Navigation Bar:

Logo (CasaGen)

Links: Features | Pricing | Blog

Buttons: Log In (for existing users) | Start Free Trial (Primary CTA)

Main Headline (H1): Big, bold, and value-focused.

Example: Find Off-Market Deals. Analyze Investments. Manage Your Entire Pipeline. All with AI.

Sub-Headline: A 1-2 sentence explanation.

Example: CasaGen is the all-in-one platform for real estate professionals. Our AI specialists find leads you can't, our copilot analyzes deals in seconds, and our dashboard manages your transactions from start to close.

Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons:

Start Your 14-Day Free Trial (Primary)

Request a Demo (Secondary)

Visual:

Modern approach: A high-quality, auto-playing video (muted) that shows the most impressive parts of your product: the AI Lead Gen Chat and the AI Property Analysis dashboard. This is far more effective than a static image.

[Section 2: Social Proof]
Goal: Build instant trust. Show that other professionals are already using your tool.

Key Components:

Simple Headline: "Trusted by top agents and investors at:"

Logo Bar: A scrolling or static bar of well-known real estate brokerage logos (e.g., Compass, Keller Williams, RE/MAX, eXp Realty, etc.).

[Section 3: Feature 1 - AI Lead Generation (The "Why You're Different")]
Goal: Highlight your most unique feature: the AI Lead Gen chat.

Layout: A two-column layout.

Left Side (Text):

Headline: Find Leads No One Else Can See.

Description: "Stop competing for the same public listings. Our AI Lead Specialist sifts through tax records, owner history, and proxy data to find hidden, off-market opportunities. Just ask in plain English."

Checklist:

✓ Find long-term owners

✓ Identify likely investment properties

✓ Uncover out-of-state owners

A simple, animated GIF of the "CasaGen Lead Generation Chat" in action (exactly like the screenshots you sent). It should show a user typing a query and the AI returning qualified leads.

Headline: Your Entire Business, All in One Place.

Checklist:

✓ Automated transaction timelines

✓ Curated market news feed

A clean, professional composite image showing the "Transactions" page timeline next to the new "Calendar" view, with lines/arrows showing how a task from one appears on the other.
